Biography

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as the "Missile Man of India," was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a pivotal role in India's Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998 and was deeply involved in the nation’s space and missile development programs. Known for his humility, vision, and inspirational teachings, Dr. Kalam was a teacher at heart who loved to connect with the youth of the country. His books like *Wings of Fire* and *Ignited Minds* continue to motivate millions. He remains one of India's most beloved presidents and a role model for generations.
